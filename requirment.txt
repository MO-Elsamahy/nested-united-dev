Based on the contract provided, you are tasked with completing a **Web Platform** consisting of three integrated systems: **HR, Accounting, and CRM**. The contract explicitly outlines the technical specifications in "Annex 1" (Pages 4-6).

Here is the breakdown of the features you are contractually required to deliver to the client.

### 1. HR Management System (HRMS)

This system focuses on employee management, attendance, and payroll.

* 
**Admin Dashboard:** Must display real-time statistics (attendance/absence, pending requests) and allow the publication of announcements to employee dashboards.


* 
**Employee Database:** A comprehensive record of employee data, salaries, and leave balances.


* **Attendance (Critical Logic):**
* 
**Mechanism:** A "Smart Button" in the employee's web account for Check-in/Check-out.


* 
**Constraint:** You must use **Server Time** exclusively to prevent manipulation; client-side time is not acceptable.




* **Payroll Engine:**
* 
**Formula:** Automated calculation: (Basic + Allowances + Overtime) - (Deductions - Absence).


* 
**Export:** Must export the final payroll sheet to **Excel**, including the employee name, net salary, and transfer details.




* 
**Requests Workflow:** A cycle for submitting and approving/rejecting requests (Leave, Advance, Mission).



### 2. Accounting System

The contract specifies that this must be built on **Double-Entry** standards, following logic similar to global systems like **Odoo**.

* **Financial Structure:**
* Flexible Chart of Accounts.


* Independent Journals (Sales, Purchases, Cash, Bank).


* 
**Cost Centers:** Required to link expenses to specific projects.




* **Purchases & Expenses:**
* Supplier invoice recording with detailed line items.


* 
**File Upload:** Must allow uploading/attaching the original invoice (Image/PDF).




* **Required Reports:**
* 
**Partner Ledger:** Detailed statement for Customers/Suppliers showing movements and Running Balance.


* 
**General Ledger:** Movement statement for any financial account.


* 
**Financial Statements:** Trial Balance, Income Statement, and Balance Sheet.





### 3. CRM (Sales & Client Management)

* 
**Sales Pipeline:** Track client status through specific stages: (New  Contacting  Agreed  Paid  Completed  Cancelled).


* 
**Duplicate Protection:** Automatic alert if a phone number is entered that already exists in the database.


* 
**History:** A timeline view showing the history of interactions with the client.



### 4. Integration & Architecture

* 
**HR-Accounting Link:** When the HR payroll is approved, the system must **automatically generate a journal entry** in the Accounting system for review.


* **User Roles:** You must implement three distinct permission levels:
1. Admin/HR
2. Accountant
3. Employee.




* **Platform:** It must be a **Web Platform** (accessible via browser). Native mobile apps are not required.



### 5. What is Strictly OUT of Scope

Do **not** spend time developing the following, as they are explicitly excluded:

* 
**Native Mobile Apps** (iOS/Android).


* 
**Hardware Integration:** No fingerprint scanners or biometric devices.


* 
**Payment & SMS:** No integration with Bank Payment Gateways or SMS services.


* 
**Data Entry:** You are not responsible for entering initial data (e.g., employee names or opening balances).
